Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2025

InterfaceForge ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data the app handles, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. InterfaceForge does not require an account, does not track you, and does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or attribution SDKs.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • API key — stored in the iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
  • API endpoint URL — stored in UserDefaults
  • Design history & prompt library — stored in UserDefaults and optionally synced via your private iCloud container
  • Onboarding state & preferences — stored in UserDefaults
  • Usage counters — stored in UserDefaults for free-tier rate limiting

3. Data Sent to Third Parties

When you generate a UI component, your prompt is sent to the AI endpoint you have configured:

  • Free tier (BYOK) — prompts are sent directly to the API endpoint you provide (e.g., OpenAI). We never see or relay these requests.
  • Pro tier (hosted AI) — prompts are sent to our Cloudflare Worker proxy, which validates your App Store subscription receipt and forwards the request to OpenAI. We do not log prompts or generated content. The receipt is verified against Apple's servers; we do not store it.

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties for advertising, marketing, or data mining.

4. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your design history and prompt library may sync via Apple's CloudKit in your private iCloud container. This data is encrypted by Apple and is not accessible to us.

The Community Gallery uses CloudKit's public database. Designs you explicitly share to the gallery are visible to other InterfaceForge users.

5. Screenshot Import

When you use the Screenshot → Component feature, the selected image is sent to the AI provider (your own endpoint or our proxy for Pro users) for visual analysis. The image is not stored by us.

6. Children's Privacy

InterfaceForge is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user.

7. App Store & Apple

Apple may collect usage data related to app downloads, crashes, and subscriptions through their own systems. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. All app data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud container. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data in iOS Settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@shopheck.com or visit our support page.
